      AGASARO INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY (AIA) wishes to welcome all parents, tutors, as well as partners, for an enriching visit at our brand new location, which hosts our Nursery and Primary School. Our premises provide a welcoming, happy, safe, and supportive learning environment in which your child will thrive and develop into a well-rounded, confident and responsible individual aspiring to achieve his or her full potential.
