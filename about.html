      Our story and how we have started

      Agasaro international academy is a blended bilingual (English and French) international school. The birth of AGASARO International Academy sprung from the desire of its founders to create an environment where child wellness met world class education. Considering the multiple socio-economic changes and the rapid development taking place in Rwanda, the founders realized that future genera-tions would need to be equipped with excellent writing and oral skills in addition to other academic credentials. Thus was born AGASARO, which we hope is the future pearl of the Rwandan education system. AGASARO is currently composed of a nursery and primary school, with plans to expand to secondary school in the foreseeable future.

      Vision

      AGASARO International Academy aspires to champion a passionate, inspired community of students prepared to continue their intellectual, emotional and physical development

      Mission

      AGASARO International Academy aims to provide all student with a school environment at par with international standards by applying the Cambridge international curriculum throughout all courses. Using a combination of proven and advanced learning techniques from a team of highly skilled teaching staff.

      Quality education

      Quality education based on the Cambridge Curriculum, through creative learning and with the help of a dynamic teaching staff Ten main course subjects, which include Math, Science, Art & Design, IT Starters, Physical Education, Digital Literacy, Music and Global Perspectives

      Goal

      provide students with rich learning mate, with a strong emphasis on strong written and oral skills In French and English. TO promote the love for STEM (Science Technology Engineering math) fields at an early age, through Insightful, creative, and tun activities inside and outside of the classroom.
