      Announcement and BASIC INFORMATION AND REQUIREMENT

      DEAR PARENTS, Agasaro International Academy team is excited to receive children for the next (2021 - 2022) academic year. We look forward to fostering positive relationships and pursuing high levels of collaboration and partnership in education of our children. Our school calendar Click here

      What We Offer:

      Cambridge International Assessment education Curriculum (Anglophone Curriculum)

      DELF: Diplôme Elémentaire de la Langue Française (Francophone Curriculum)

      The school has 3 sections

      a. Full nursery

      b. Full Primary

      c. Lower Secondary

      School fees:

      Nursery: 260.000 Frw N1 to N3 per term per child

      Primary : 280.000 Frw G1 to G6 per term per child

      Secondary one : 350.000 Frw per term per child

      School materials :

      Nursery : 50.000 Frw per Year per child Text books and note books only

      Primary : 100.000 Frw per Year per child Text books and note books only

      Secondary : 120.000 Frw Per year per child Text books and note books only

      School uniform And Other Materials:

      Uniform Nursery : 50,000 Frw per year per child

      Uniform Primary : 55,000 Frw per year per child

      Uniform Secondary : 70,000 Frw per year per child

      School lunch : 77,500 Frw per term per child

      School bus: 40,000 Frw per child monthly

      Registration : 20,000 Frw per year per child School Insurance : 1,000 Frw per Year per child

      Registration Processes

      You can visit our school Schedule here

      Nursery requirements are:

      Birth certificate or any health insurance card for nursery 1

      Have paid the registration fee

      Be at least 3 years old maxim for the first Kindergarten year.

      Registration in medium and top classes of nursery for children from other schools, needs academic supporting document.

      Primary and lower secondary requirements are:

      Provide academic supports (School report card, Certificate, Transcript) from previous school.

      An entrance test is always submitted to new candidates coming from non-international schools.

      Once the result is confirmed, the concerned student must present the registration fee slip.

      School Feeding and School bus
      • The children take food (breakfast and Lunch) at school
      • The parents who want children to use the school bus, they must register monthly the child/children..
      Parent-school meetings
      • The school organizes the meeting with parents once at the end of each term.t
      • For 2021 – 2022 academic year, the parents who will be participating in meetings organized by the school, will present the valid COVID-19 test result.
      • Parent can take an appointment with child’s subject teacher once a month for education purposes. The school administration must be informed 48 hours before the process to be done.
      • Internal rules and regulations which guide the global and good functioning of the school will be shared in hard copy with all parents at the beginning of this academic year.
      • School administration will be informing to the parents the improvised events which are not indicated on school calendar
